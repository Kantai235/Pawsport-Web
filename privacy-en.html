Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

繁體中文 English

AT A GLANCE

We do not collect, upload, or sell your data. Your imported content and settings stay on your device for Pawsport and its widget.

Pawsport (the “App”) is provided by Kantai Developer. This policy explains how we handle information when you use Pawsport.

1. We do not collect your personal data

Pawsport does not collect, upload, sell, or share your personal data. The App has no account system and does not include advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or third-party cloud-sync services.

The images, videos, canvas content, and widget images that you import are stored locally on your device or in a local App Group shared container. They are used only to display content in the App and its widget. We do not send this content to any server.

2. Photo library and file access

When you choose to import media, Pawsport uses the iOS system photo picker or Files app to let you select images and videos. The App processes only the content you explicitly select, for display, slideshows, canvas editing, or widget display.

You may choose not to grant photo-library access or not to select any files. Other features that do not require imported media remain available.

3. On-device use of device state

Pawsport may use device orientation to help rotate full-screen presentations and may read the battery state to show low-battery alerts. This information is used in real time on your device only; it is not stored, uploaded, or shared.

4. Third-party services and tracking

The current version does not include analytics services, advertising services, tracking SDKs, third-party cloud-sync services, or cross-app tracking. Pawsport does not build behavioural profiles or provide data to advertisers or data brokers.

5. Your data and deletion

You can delete imported media, canvases, or widget images in the App. When you do, Pawsport removes the related local content. You can also delete the App to remove its local App data. Because we do not hold your data on a server, there is no server-side deletion request to make.

6. Changes to this policy

If a future version adds cloud sync, accounts, analytics, payments, advertising, or any other feature that changes how data is handled, we will update this policy and its last-updated date before that change takes effect.

7. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or how Pawsport handles data, please contact us through the Support & contact page. To protect your privacy, please do not post identity documents, addresses, or other sensitive personal information in public reports.
